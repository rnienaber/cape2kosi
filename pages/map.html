Diary (map based)

The hikers have been sending through their GPS coordinates every evening and each desination is shown by a marker on the map.

Red marker shows where they ended each day.

Blue marker shows the rest days. (While they are the same spot at the previous day, they’ve been moved slightly so they don’t overlap.)

Green marker shows where they started and finished!


Click on any marker to see more details about that day and to find a link to the diary for that day.
